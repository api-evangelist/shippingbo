Connecting Sage ERP to a WMS addresses the limitations of using an ERP alone for logistics operations. While a direct Sage-to-WMS connection works for simple environments, more complex operations benefit from a Sage plus OMS plus WMS architecture that separates commercial management, order orchestration, and warehouse execution into specialized roles.
