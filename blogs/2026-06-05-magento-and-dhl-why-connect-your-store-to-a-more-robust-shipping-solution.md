While connecting Magento directly to DHL carrier streamlines basic shipping tasks like label generation and order dispatch, this direct integration becomes insufficient as e-commerce volumes grow. Shippingbo proposes adding an orchestration layer combining OMS and TMS to centralize orders, inventory, and multi-carrier management across the entire logistics workflow.
